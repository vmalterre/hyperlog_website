Why Hyperledger Fabric?

HyperLog chose Hyperledger Fabric over public blockchains like Ethereum for critical reasons:

No cryptocurrency. No tokens, no gas fees, no price volatility. Pure enterprise infrastructure.

Permissioned network. Known participants, regulatory compliance, and enterprise security requirements met.

Scalability. Handles thousands of transactions per second without the limitations of public networks.

Privacy. Channel-based architecture allows selective data sharing between authorized parties.
