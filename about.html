Our Story

HyperLog was founded with a simple observation: in an age of digital transformation, pilots still rely on paper logbooks and centralized databases that they don't control.

Lost logbooks can derail careers. Disputed hours can cost jobs. Verification processes can take weeks. We knew there had to be a better way.

By combining enterprise blockchain technology with aviation expertise, we're building the next generation pilot logbook‚Äîone where every entry is immutable, every signature is cryptographic, and every pilot owns their data.
